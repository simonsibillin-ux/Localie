Frequently asked questions

What happens if my application is rejected? ▼
If your application doesn't meet our standards, we'll explain why and give you guidance on what to improve. We're selective by design — every provider on Localie is personally vetted. If you're rejected, we can discuss ways to reapply when you're ready.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes, absolutely. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access ends at the end of your billing period.
What counts as a post? ▼
A post is any content you share to your business profile — photos, service updates, announcements, promotions, or availability changes. Posts help you stay top-of-mind with customers. Different tiers allow different posting frequencies per week.
How do referral credits work? ▼
When you refer another business to Localie and they're approved, you earn referral credits. These credits can be applied to your monthly subscription bill, reducing what you owe. It's our way of rewarding you for helping grow the Localie community.
Is there a setup fee? ▼
No setup fees, no hidden charges. You just pay the monthly or annual subscription price. Your first 14 days are free — no credit card required. After that, you'll be charged according to your chosen plan.
What is the vetting process? ▼
We verify your ABN, check your current insurance, and review your business reputation. This takes 2–5 business days. We're thorough because customers need to trust that every provider on Localie is legitimate, insured, and accountable.
